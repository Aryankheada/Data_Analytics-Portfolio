PROJECT: Ecommerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

Objective:
Analyze ecommerce sales performance using SQL and Excel to identify revenue trends, customer behavior, top-performing products, city-wise performance, and business growth patterns.

Tools Used:

* MySQL
* Microsoft Excel

Dataset:
Ecommerce sales dataset containing customer, order, order_items, product, payment, and return information.

SQL Concepts Used:

* INNER JOIN
* GROUP BY
* Aggregate Functions (SUM, COUNT, AVG)
* CTE (Common Table Expressions)
* Window Functions (DENSE_RANK, OVER, LAG)
* CASE WHEN
* DATE_FORMAT
* ORDER BY
* LIMIT

Business Questions Solved:

1. Overall KPI Summary
2. Monthly Revenue Trend
3. Monthly Orders Trend
4. Top Cities by Revenue
5. Top Products by Revenue
6. Worst Performing Products
7. Category Revenue Contribution %
8. Top Category by Month
9. Customer Segmentation
10. Repeat vs One-Time Customers
11. Top Customers by City
12. Customers Spending Above City Average
13. Payment Analysis
14. Return Analysis
15. Brand/Seller Performance
16. City/State Sales Performance
17. Month-over-Month Revenue Growth

Key KPIs:

* Total Orders: 6,977
* Total Revenue: ₹261 Cr
* Total Customers: 1,945
* Average Order Value: ₹3.75 Lakh
* Total Quantity Sold: 1,14,010

Key Insights:

* Chennai generated the highest revenue among all cities.
* Monthly revenue and order trends showed overall business stability.
* A small number of products contributed significantly to overall revenue.
* Revenue contribution was concentrated in a few high-performing categories.
* Customer spending behavior varied significantly across cities.
* High-value customers contributed disproportionately to total sales.
* Certain brands/products consistently outperformed others.
* Return patterns highlighted areas of potential business improvement.

Dashboard Features:

* KPI Cards
* Monthly Revenue Trend
* Monthly Orders Trend
* Top Cities by Revenue
* Top Products by Revenue

Outcome:
Built an end-to-end Ecommerce Sales Analysis project using SQL and Excel to analyze business performance and create a professional dashboard for decision-making.

Future Scope:

* Rebuild dashboard in Power BI with interactive filters and slicers
* Perform advanced data analysis using Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
* Add predictive analytics and customer segmentation models
